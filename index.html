Welcome to Fakepoll.

A website designed to generate poll responses based on pre-determined criteria. In other words, it will make responses appear out of thin air, which you can then use for school projects.

This is meant to help provide irrelevant tangible data for school projects, and other things of that nature. Please be responsible, don't use this to generate absolute b#llsh*t!
